El Pollo Loco

Story

Pepe needs to find the biggest chicken for a party so everyone gets fed. Unfortunately that chicken is still alive and being protected by many chicks and chickens. Kill them by a jump or by throwing bottles at them. Be aware the big chicken gets only defeated by bottles. So save some for later!

Desktop

Use the arrow keys to move the character. Use the "D" to throw

Mobile device

Touch the arrows to move and the bottle to throw
